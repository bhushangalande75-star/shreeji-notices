Last updated: April 2026 · Compliant with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA)

What data we collect

The following personal data is collected and stored when you use the Member Portal:

  • Identity data: Your name, flat number, building number
  • Contact data: Mobile number, email address (if provided)
  • Financial data: Maintenance notice amounts, payment status, and payment history
  • Usage data: Login timestamps, IP address, chatbot interactions
  • Complaint data: Tickets and complaints raised through the portal

Why we collect it

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To manage your society membership and maintenance notices
  • To process payments and send receipts
  • To communicate society announcements and decisions
  • To resolve complaints and service requests
  • To comply with Maharashtra Co-operative Societies Act 1960 requirements

Who has access

Your personal data is accessible only to:

  • Society committee members who are registered on the platform
  • Platform administrators for support and maintenance purposes

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data storage and security

All data is stored on encrypted PostgreSQL databases hosted on Neon (cloud, ap-southeast-1 region). Access is protected by TLS encryption in transit and at rest. Authentication uses bcrypt password hashing. All login and access events are logged.

Your rights under DPDPA 2023

Right to Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you by contacting the society office.

Right to Correction

If your name, phone, or email is incorrect, contact the society office to update it.

Right to Erasure

You may request deletion of your personal data. This will remove your portal access, payment history, and personal identifiers.

Right to Grievance

If you believe your data has been misused, raise a complaint with the society secretary or contact the Data Protection Board of India.

Data retention

Personal data is retained for the duration of your society membership plus 2 years as required by applicable laws. Financial records (notice and payment history) are retained for 7 years per statutory requirements.

Request data deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data from the portal below. Note that statutory financial records (notice history) cannot be deleted due to legal requirements, but all personally identifiable information will be anonymised.

Contact

For any privacy-related queries, contact the society secretary. For DPDPA complaints, contact the Data Protection Board of India.
